Paul Margolis

President

Paul Margolis is President of Exel. Paul has had a successful career in technology companies as Founder, CEO of public and private companies, venture capitalist, private equity investor, and investment manager.

Paul co-founded Marcam Corporation, an ERP software company, and served as its Chairman and CEO for 15 years. Marcam grew to over $200 million in revenue, with more than 1,200 employees and operations around the world. Marcam had a successful IPO and Paul was CEO as a public company for five years.

Paul also co-founded Longworth Venture Partners, a successful software venture capital firm. Longworth has invested in 60 software companies over 20 years. Paul has personally led or co-led investments in more than 30 software companies and has served on the board of directors of 27 software companies, including three public companies.
